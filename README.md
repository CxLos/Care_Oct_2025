## üìù Description 

An interactive Plotly/Dash dashboard that tracks and visualizes care network activities at the Black Men's Health Clinic (BMHC). ## üß™ Methodology

The data for this project was sourced directly from the Black Men's Health Clinic

### Plans for collected data:

- Track care coordination trends to identify effective service delivery patterns
- Monitor patient health outcomes to measure care network effectiveness
- Analyze referral pathways to optimize healthcare partner relationships
- Generate automated reports for healthcare providers and stakeholders
- Identify gaps in service coverage and opportunities for care expansion

## üîç Findings

### Care Network Engagement

* Total care network interactions and referrals completed
* Patient enrollment and participation rates
* Geographic distribution of care network services

### Service Delivery Metrics

* Types of care services provided (primary care, specialist referrals, mental health, etc.)
* Patient satisfaction scores and feedback
* Follow-up appointment completion rates

### Partnership & Coordination

* Healthcare provider partnerships and collaborations
* Referral network efficiency and response times
* Community resource connections and integration

## ‚úÖ Conclusion

The Care Network Activity Report provides valuable insights into the effectiveness of BMHC's care coordination and patient service delivery. The data collected enables leadership to make informed decisions about healthcare partnerships, service expansion, and patient care strategies. By tracking key health outcomes and engagement metrics, this dashboard supports continuous improvement in care quality and patient satisfaction. Regular monitoring of these metrics helps ensure that care network operations effectively serve the health needs of Black men in the community and strengthen connections between patients and essential healthcare resources. This data-driven approach continues to advance BMHC's mission of improving health equity and access to quality care.
